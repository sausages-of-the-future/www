Services can be provided by central, local and devolved government, and by commercial and third sector organisations.

The infrastructure looks something like this

Services

Far simpler, more focused & diverse, dynamic, overlapping. Public services run by private/public/3rd sector. Commercial services also supported by this digital civic infrastructure.

Common Platforms

Payment, publishing,identity assurance, geospatial, addresses, mapping, notification, secure messaging etc.Centrally hosted or SaaS or open source.

Trust & Consent

Independent scrutiny so data (open & private) is managed to maintain users’ trust. Responsible for data quality, security, informed consent.

Registers

Foundations of a digital state. Canonical data, managed by Registrars who serve users of all services – public & private.
