Background and Findings
We have a lot to learn

Aims and objectives

To develop a longer-term vision for digital government beyond the next Parliament.
The UK has an opportunity to deliver better and more joined-up public services, for less, whilst stimulating innovation and new partnerships. In order to enable this we must put in place bold new digital civic infrastructure and build greater service delivery capability in the civil service.
